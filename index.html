About Us

We sincerely believes in comprehensively training the students to face the corporate world and have a successful professional career. The training programs encompass a three stage process to train the students to make them ready for the industry and develop the students to face the corporate world. Special emphasis is made to train the students to meet the requirements of the information technology industry through e-learning. This facilitates the students to learn technologies of the information technology industry in a scientific manner through a structured path, on 24X7 basis, at a pace suitable for individual students. Our college has entered in to a tie up with an industry leader for the delivery of this program. Almost, all the eligible students have been placed in reputed companies with some of them having more than one offer. The Mission of our Training and Placement Cell is to train the students on industry needs and provide best of opportunities available in India and abroad for its students. The Goal of Training and Placement Cell is to provide career guidance and placement opportunities to 100% students studying

The Training And Placement Cell contains all the information about the students. The system stores all the personal information of the students, like their personal details, their aggregate marks, their skill set and their technical skills that are required in the CV to be sent to a company.
